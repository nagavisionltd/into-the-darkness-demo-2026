Early Rooms (1-3):
- Light hits only (0.1x knockback)
- Teach: Basic attacks, positioning

Mid Rooms (4-7):
- Medium hits (0.5x knockback)
- Teach: Knockback into hazards (+20 EC)
- Combo windows: 280ms (Lord Soul standard)

Late Rooms (8-11):
- Heavy hits (1.0x knockback)
- Teach: Launch combos (2.5x finisher)
- Just frame bonuses: +10% damage

Boss Room (13):
- Full combo system
- Hit stop: 50-100ms on heavy attacks
- Particle bursts: 12-20 per hit
- Screen shake: 0.006-0.008 intensity
